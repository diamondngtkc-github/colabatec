Déjà Vu at Arc Gallery

Côlab is proud to present Déjà Vu, the juried art show at ARC gallery!

This immersive group exhibition dives deep into the themes of time, memory, and perception, featuring a diverse range of innovative contemporary artworks — from interactive installations and video art to painting and performance.

📅 Don't Miss the Opening Reception:
Friday, November 7th, 2025  |  7–9 PM
📍 ARC Gallery  |  2602 Main St, Dallas, TX
🎙 SPECIAL EVENT: Sound Interactive Performance @ 8:00 PM
The exhibition runs through January 8, 2026, featuring works juried by Professor Marilyn Waligore.

Featuring the work of 11 talented artists: Anisha Chaudhary, Amanda Dunbar, Betül Sertkaya, Diamond Nguyen, Daniel Kim, Kristen Duong, Monica Berlanga, Lauren E. Allen, Scott Tucker & Baotran Vo, and Vaji Zamaniderkaani.

See you there!
